Shareholder Proposal: Shareholder Bio

Shareholder of Record

Dr. David Levitt

David Levitt is a software entrepreneur, activist and writer. He is CEO of technology developer and app maker Levity Novelty LLC.

Dr. Levitt has developed award winning and innovative software products for the Mac and acclaimed apps for the iPad and iPhone including Invisibility 3D. He received his BS from Yale at age 20 and his doctorate in Artiﬁcial Intelligence from MIT. His technical activities include research, software development and teaching.

A lifelong advocate of civil liberties and civil rights, Dr. Levitt was nominated by California voters in 2012 to run for US Senate, opposing bulk surveillance advocate and war proﬁteer Senator Dianne Feinstein. Levitt received numerous endorsements and in San Francisco came in second out of 24 candidates in the primary.

Dr. Levitt is San Francisco bay area liaison for Restore the Fourth, which defends our US constitutional rights to be free from unreasonable search. A long standing Apple user, developer and stockholder, Levitt introduced the Spy Lockout shareholder proposal, to be introduced at Apple’s February 27, 2014 shareholder meeting.
